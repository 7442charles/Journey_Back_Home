About Us

Our Story

A Journey Back Home is more than a brand — it’s a cultural movement, a soulful reconnection with our roots, and a celebration of African identity. Born from the heartbeat of the continent, our vision is to guide modern souls on a meaningful return — whether spiritual, cultural, or physical — from the cold isolation of foreign lands (“ice”) back to the vibrant warmth of Africa (“safari”).

We tell stories etched in tradition, boldness, and pride. Through our visuals, products, and experiences, we blend heritage and modern expression — reconnecting generations with a sense of belonging, pride, and purpose.

Our Mission

To inspire reconnection with African identity through powerful storytelling, cultural expression, and meaningful engagement with the continent.

Our Vision

To become a global bridge between the diaspora and Africa — turning memories, art, and history into living movements.

THIS IS WHAT WE STAND FOR

Cultural Pride   |   Authentic Storytelling   |   Unity Across Borders   |   Empowerment Through Identity
