About Us

Our Story

I was born at the foot of Mount Kenya, in a humble village called Nanyuki — a place of deep beauty and deep struggle. My childhood was not filled with toys or luxury, but with resilience.

As a young girl, I walked barefoot to school — over 30 minutes each way, on rocky paths, often with an empty stomach, but with a head full of hope. Our clothes smelled of firewood smoke, because that’s how we cooked — outside, with wood we gathered ourselves.

And that firewood? We didn’t just pick it nearby. We walked deep into the forest, risking everything. Sometimes we came face to face with wild elephants. I remember running for our lives, firewood on our backs, hearts pounding, praying to make it home.

We’d stop by the river to drink water dripping down the rocks from the mountain — because that was all we had. That water tasted like survival.

Yet even with so little… I dreamed big.

I later joined beauty school and became Miss Beauty Point Kenya. I went on to compete in Miss Universe Kenya — not because I had perfect confidence, but because I had a burning desire to be seen, to share the beauty of my country with the world.

But not everyone clapped for me. I’ve been bullied. Laughed at. Talked about. Dismissed. Other women told me I could never do anything worthy in life. They tried to make me small, tried to silence me.

But little did they know… their doubt became my fuel.

Today, I live in Iceland. I married a wonderful Icelandic man and started my own nail studio. It was in that tiny studio, as I did nails and shared stories of Kenya with my clients, that something magical happened — they listened. They became curious. They began to dream, too.

That’s when I knew: This was bigger than me. This was destiny. That’s when Journey Back Home was born.

This trip isn’t just about travel. It’s about coming full circle — from the forests of my childhood to the dreams I’m now living. It’s about showing other women that you can come from nothing and still shine. That Africa is not too far. Kenya is not too expensive. That the girl who used to walk barefoot in ash-smelling clothes is now inviting the world to walk beside her — not just as a guide, but as a living testimony.

To every woman who has ever been told she’s not enough — this is for you. Let’s go back. Not just to Kenya — but to the heart.

Our Mission

From Ice to Safari was born from a dream — to share the magic of Kenya with those who seek more than just adventure. Our mission is to offer deeply rooted, culturally immersive travel that inspires, empowers, and uplifts. We bring people together across borders, backgrounds, and beliefs — to share laughter, stories, sunsets, and soul. Because travel should not only change the way you see the world — it should change the way you see yourself.

Our Vision

To open hearts, one journey at a time. Our dream is to create a travel experience where every guest feels welcomed, enriched, and empowered — whether they’re stepping into the wild beauty of a Kenyan safari or the quiet strength of a village sunset. We see travel as a tool for unity, healing, and transformation — and Kenya as the perfect place to begin that story.

Cultural Pride   |   Authentic Storytelling   |   Unity Across Borders   |   Empowerment Through Identity
