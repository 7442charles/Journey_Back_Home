Detailed Itinerary

Day 1 - Welcome to Africa

Step off the plane into warm African sunshine. Traditional dancers greet you with vibrant drums and joy — a true celebration of your arrival. We check in at a beautiful boutique hotel in Nairobi — your peaceful base to rest, smile, and prepare for a journey filled with culture, connection, and awe.

Day 2 – Up Close With Giants & Souls That Shine

We wake up to the golden glow of Nairobi and enjoy a warm breakfast as the city gently stirs to life. Today, we begin a journey that touches the heart and awakens the spirit.

First stop: the Giraffe Center, where you’ll share eye-level kisses and feed towering giraffes with your own hands — a magical connection you’ll never forget. Then, we head to the Sheldrick Wildlife Trust, where rescued baby elephants will melt your heart with their playful charm and powerful stories of survival.

But the day doesn’t stop there. We continue straight to a local children’s home, where laughter, love, and strength echo through every corner. It’s a humbling and uplifting moment — one that reminds us why we travel, and how much kindness exists in the world.
